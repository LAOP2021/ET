Would you like to submit a paper?

To submit a paper, please visit our Submission Site.

Submissions are limited to 10 pages in the IEEE format (see https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html). The 10-page limit includes figures, tables, and your appendices, but does not include references, for which there is no page limit.

WORKS papers this year will be published in cooperation with TCHPC and that they will be available from IEEE digital repository.
